## Monospace Bar Chart

Terminal-style KPI header plus a single-series bar chart. Bars **load expanded**, **shrink to thin** on intro, then **expand on hover** (same interaction as nqchart monospace). Use `<Bar variant="monospace" />` on `NQBarChart`. Square corners via `barRadius={0}`.
